The Haven Raises $3,750

June 30th, 2009

A winning team of five ladies from The Haven of Our Lady of Peace, Pensacola, Florida raised $3,750 in their fundraiser with the Pensacola Pelicans baseball team in the benefit game, “Knocking Poverty Out of the Park” on June 10, 2009.

Presentation at the Ball Game

The Haven team from left-to-right are: Laura Ayottte, Martha Perez, Jocelyn Longo, Linda Irby, and Tara Root.

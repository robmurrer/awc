Donate

If you wish to make a donation to the Society's Alfred-Washburn Center, you can click on the Donate button if you would like to give electronically with a credit card. You can also make checks payable to the Alfred-Washburn Center and mail it to address below:

Alfred-Washburn Center

PO Box 17645

Pensacola, Florida 32522

You are also invited to bring your donations to the Center in Person.

The Center also accepts the following donations:

Personal Hygiene

  • Deodorant and lotion
  • Feminine Hygiene Products
  • Tooth brushes and small tubes of tooth paste

Supplies

  • Paper towels
  • Bleach
  • Toilet paper
  • Recycled plastic grocery bags
  • Laundry soap (HE Liquid Only

Bycycles:

  • Used Bicycles
  • 20' - 26' Tubes
  • Bike Locks

Food Items

  • Peanut butter
  • Jelly
  • Coffee
  • Sugar
  • Coffee Creamer
  • Individual size juice packs
  • Small cans of Beans and Franks (7.75 oz)
  • Small cans of Vienna Sausage (5 oz)
  • Individual  packages of cookies and crackers
  • Ramen Noodles

Miscellaneous  

  • Cold Weather Supplies
  • Tents
  • Tarps
  • Sleeping bags
  • Blankets

 

The St. Vincent dePaul Society's Alfred-Washburn Center is a tax exempt 501 (c) (3)  organization. All donations received by the AWC will be acknowledged with a Thank You letter.

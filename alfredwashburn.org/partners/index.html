Partners

The Alfred-Washburn Center is staffed by volunteers five days per week. Volunteers come from local  churches and organizations in our community. There are many individuals, organizations and churches that provide significant support, both financial and services, to serve the homeless. These include:

  • American Legion, Frank Marston Post 33
  • East Brent Baptist Church
  • Cokesbury United Methodist Church
  • Disabled American Veterans, Al Gray Chapter #23
  • Emmanuel  Sheppard & Condon (ESC)
  • Greater True Vine Missionary Baptist Church
  • Haven of Our Lady of Peace
  • Holy Cross Episcopal Church
  • Jansen Quality Construction
  • Mass Kunfuzion Band
  • Nativity of Our Lord Catholic Church
  • Sacred Heart Hospital
  • St. Ann Catholic Church, Gulf Breeze
  • St. Luke United Methodist Church
  • St. Paul Catholic Church
  • Veterans of Foreign Wars, Post 23
  • Woodbine United Methodist Church
  • Our Lady of the Assumption, Catholic Church
